The Artist

Eunice (the Weeb) is the protagonist of the comic. She is an artist with a background in animation & is experienced in video editing. She also coded this website.

She has been studying Japanese since 2017 & is aiming to further her drawing skills at a vocational school in Tokyo. She likes serialized stories, fantasy worlds & eating mushrooms.

Tools: Wacom Mobile Studio Pro 13 (1st Gen) connected to PC, 8bitdo (as macropad), Clip Studio Paint EX 3.0, Canva
